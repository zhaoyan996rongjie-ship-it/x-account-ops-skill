## Search Query Patterns

### Single hashtag search:
```
https://x.com/search?q=%23Codex&src=typed_query&f=live
```

### Multiple hashtag OR search:
```
https://x.com/search?q=%23Codex+OR+%23ClaudeCode&src=typed_query&f=live
```

## Engagement Allocation
Out of 20-30 daily interactions:
- 8-10 on primary hashtag posts (likes + replies)
- 5-7 on secondary hashtag posts (likes only)
- 5-7 on broader AI community posts (likes + replies)
- 2-3 on own post replies/engagement
